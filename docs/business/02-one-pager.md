# PitchUp — One-Pager

## The Platform That Puts Mobile Food Vendors Online

**PitchUp** is a subscription platform that gives chippy vans, burger trucks, coffee carts, and mobile food vendors their own branded website with online pre-ordering, fleet management, and live location tracking — with zero commission on orders.

---

### The Problem
15,000+ UK mobile food vendors have no online ordering, no fleet tools, and no brand presence beyond Facebook. The alternatives? Give 30% to Deliveroo or do nothing.

### The Solution
PitchUp gives vendors a branded website in minutes — pre-ordering with pickup codes, a kitchen display system, multi-van route scheduling, live map presence, event management, and analytics. Customers order ahead, pay at the van.

### Why It Wins
- **Zero commission** — flat subscription, vendor keeps 100% of sales
- **Built for mobile** — not a restaurant platform shoehorned into vans
- **Multi-van fleet management** — the only platform that handles it
- **Brand-first** — vendor's site looks like *their* business
- **Free tier** — zero friction to start, natural upgrade path
