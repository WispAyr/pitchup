## Unit Economics

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) — *Estimated*

| Channel | Est. CAC | Notes |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Organic/social | £10-20 | Content marketing, food vendor communities |
| Events/trade shows | £30-50 | NCASS events, street food festivals |
| Paid social (Facebook/Instagram) | £25-40 | Targeted to food vendor pages |
| Referral | £15-25 | Existing vendor refers new vendor |
| **Blended average** | **£25** | Weighted toward organic early on |

### Lifetime Value (LTV)

| Tier | Monthly ARPU | Est. Retention | LTV |
|------|-------------|----------------|-----|
| Free → Starter conversion | £29 | 18 months avg | £522 |
| Starter | £29 + £3 addons = £32 | 24 months | £768 |
| Pro | £59 + £8 addons = £67 | 30 months | £2,010 |
| Enterprise | £99 + £15 addons = £114 | 36 months | £4,104 |
| **Blended (paid)** | **£45** | **24 months** | **£1,080** |

### LTV:CAC Ratio
- **Blended:** £1,080 / £25 = **43:1** *(exceptional, likely to normalise as paid acquisition scales)*
- **Realistic at scale:** Targeting 10:1+ as CAC increases with paid channels

### Churn Assumptions — *Estimated*
- **Monthly churn (paid):** 3-5% (food vendors are seasonal; some churn in winter)
- **Annual churn:** ~35-45%
- **Mitigation:** Annual plans (lock-in), seasonal pause feature (future), event tie-ins

## Break-Even Analysis

### Fixed Costs (Estimated Monthly)

| Cost | Monthly |
|------|---------|
| Hosting/infrastructure | £200 |
| Domain/SSL/CDN | £50 |
| Email/SMS services | £50 |
| Ewan's time (opportunity cost) | £3,000 |
| Marketing spend | £200-1,000 |
| **Total (lean)** | **£3,500** |
| **Total (with marketing)** | **£4,500** |

### Break-Even Points

| Scenario | Paying Vendors Needed | At Blended ARPU £42/mo |
|----------|----------------------|------------------------|
| Lean (£3,500/mo) | **84 vendors** | Month 10-14 (moderate) |
| With marketing (£4,500/mo) | **107 vendors** | Month 12-18 (moderate) |
| Excluding founder time (£500/mo) | **12 vendors** | Month 2-4 |

*Excluding founder opportunity cost, PitchUp breaks even extremely quickly — it's a software product with near-zero marginal cost per vendor.*

### Cash Flow Note
Annual plans provide upfront cash. At 40% annual uptake:
- 100 paying vendors × 40% annual × avg £430/yr = **£17,200 upfront cash** in Year 1
