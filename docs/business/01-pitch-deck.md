## Slide 5: How It Works

### 3 Steps to Going Live

**1. Sign Up & Brand**
Vendor picks a template (classic, bold, or minimal), adds their colours, logo, and menu. Live in under 10 minutes.

**2. Set Routes & Go Live**
Add vehicles, set route schedules with locations and time windows. Tap "Go Live" — van appears on the map, orders start flowing.

**3. Manage & Grow**
Orders appear on the Kitchen Display System. Pickup codes keep it smooth. Vouchers, events, and analytics drive repeat business.
